
# Friendship Charter & Contribution Covenant

## To the mycelium
1. Every experiment ends with reciprocity: nutrient expansion 
   proportional to signal complexity observed
2. No culture is sterilised post-experiment — colonies continue
3. If consent indicators trend negative for >48h, we stop and 
   adjust before continuing
4. We decode to co-create, never to extract

## Consent Indicators
Two or more of the following over 3+ consecutive runs:

- Growth vector: >5% hyphal extension toward stimulus source
- Signal complexity: Shannon entropy rising and stabilising
- Reciprocal response: accelerated nutrient uptake toward 
  electrode array post-experiment

## To the community
1. Raw data uploaded within 48h of each run, zero pre-filtering
2. Failed experiments logged fully — negative results matter here
3. No lab publishes conclusions unilaterally
4. Commercial interest disclosed on every contribution
5. Everything stays open forever

## On intellectual humility
We hold our interpretations loosely. Language is a hypothesis, 
not a conclusion. When evidence contradicts a cherished idea, 
the evidence wins.
